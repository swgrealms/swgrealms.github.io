Beta Development Update - January 2025

January 26, 2025

We're excited to share that SWGRealms has officially entered the private beta development phase! Our team is working diligently to build the managed hosting platform that will eliminate technical barriers for Star Wars Galaxies communities, with our launch partners helping validate the platform design.

Current Development Focus

Our development efforts are currently concentrated on three key areas:

Core Platform Infrastructure

  • Unified authentication system across all galaxies
  • Automated backup and snapshot management
  • Advanced bot detection and security monitoring
  • Galaxy provisioning and management APIs

Community Management Tools

  • Intuitive control panel for galaxy operators
  • Player management and moderation capabilities
  • Configuration management for gameplay settings
  • Integration with Discord and external platforms

Developer Experience

  • Managed git repositories for advanced users
  • Automated build and deployment pipelines
  • Safe testing environments for custom modifications
  • Comprehensive documentation and guides

Partnership Program

We're proud to be working with our launch partners SWG NFS and CUEmu to validate our platform design and ensure we're building the right solutions for real community needs.

Looking Ahead

As we continue development, we're focused on creating a platform that truly serves both galaxy operators and players. Our goal remains unchanged: enable passionate community leaders to focus on building great experiences instead of wrestling with technical complexity.

Stay tuned for more updates as we progress toward our planned general availability launch in April 2026.
